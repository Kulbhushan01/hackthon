Natural Fertilizers

AI-Recommended Organic Solutions:

  • Green Manure: Leguminous crops like dhaincha, sunhemp for nitrogen fixation

Sustainable Practices

Eco-Friendly Farming Methods:

  • Crop Rotation: Alternating crops to prevent soil depletion
  • Mulching: Organic mulch (straw, leaves) for moisture retention
  • Water Harvesting: Rainwater collection and efficient irrigation
  • Zero Tillage: Minimal soil disturbance for carbon sequestration
  • Intercropping: Mixed cropping for biodiversity and pest control
  • Composting: On-farm waste conversion to nutrient-rich compost
